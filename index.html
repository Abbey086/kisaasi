About School

Welcome to Kisaasi College School

Kisaasi College School nurtures and cherishes the whole child. Emphasis is laid on excellent academic achievements, character building, participation in sports activities and leadership skills.

We are Islamic-based and welcoming to all religions, building responsible, self-driven, and critical learners in a supportive environment.
